Main Building

This is your Main Building. Here you can receive an excellent overview of your entire empire. On the right, this is the command-place where you can distribute your villagers to collect one of the five basic resources: Clay, Wood, Stone, Iron or Food.

On the bottom you can order to start working on any of your buildings. This is one of the most important place - the strategical center of your empire. It is the place where you can hide in case of an enemy attack and the room where you can negotiate strategical offers with your neighbours.

As you can see, all the plans are being made here. This is why, to reduce the time needed for improving any other building, you have to upgrade this command-post.
