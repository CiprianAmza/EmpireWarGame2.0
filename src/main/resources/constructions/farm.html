The Farm


Crop farming and cattle in Empire war provide resources that a villager could not otherwise obtain naturally in order to be self-sufficient. Even in a peaceful world, growing crops and cattle can be a renewable source of food and materials which can be helpful for in-game progression and animal breeding.

In order to create more soldiers or to upgrade your buildings, you need to expand the Farm.

Build up your own farm, complete with crops and cattle so that you can raise your Empire.
